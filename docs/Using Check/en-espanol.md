title: En español
---
Check speaks Spanish.

Tap the language switcher in the footer (EN / ES) and the whole app flips: buttons, verdicts, results, everything. Your choice sticks on that device.

Call transcripts are special. The call happens in whatever language the store speaks, usually English. If your app is in Spanish, we translate the conversation for you right on the result, and a **Translate** button appears any time a transcript isn't in your language.

Same promise in both languages: **sin respuesta = sin cargo.**
