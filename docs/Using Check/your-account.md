---
title: Your account
---
Tap the pill in the top right corner. That's you.

The account sheet shows your phone number, your plan, and two live numbers: **checks left** and **checks today**. Under that, three tabs:

- **Overview**: manage your plan, open your history, manage zones, see your scheduled auto checks (and cancel any with one tap), sign out.
- **Activity**: your week at a glance, filterable by outcome.
- **Earn**: every way to stack [free checks](doc:invite-a-friend).

How your balance works:

- **Plan checks** refill every month and are spent first.
- **Pay as you go checks** never expire and are spent after plan checks.
- A check is only spent on a definitive answer. Everything else is free, automatically.

Need to change your number? Tap the pencil next to it and verify the new one with a texted code.
