---
title: The live call
---
Tap **Check this store** and the call starts. You get to watch.

The screen walks through the real stages as they happen: ringing, working through the phone menu, transferred to the front, a person picked up, asking about your product. Then the conversation itself streams in as text, line by line, exactly as it's spoken.

Most calls land a verdict in about two minutes.

Good to know:

- **A check on the same store and product is limited to once an hour.** The shelf doesn't change that fast, so we save your check and show you the recent result instead.
- If you checked a store recently, we ask "check again?" before spending anything.
- Closing the app doesn't kill the call. Come back and the result is waiting in your history.
- Stores that can't be called (online only, like Amazon) show live online stock instead of a call button.
