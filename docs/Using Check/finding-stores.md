---
title: Finding stores
---
The home screen is built around one job: find the right store to check.

- **Tap the pin** and we list every store near you that carries your product, closest first, with a green dot when it's open right now.
- **Search by store or city** if you'd rather type.
- **The radius slider** goes 1 to 20 miles. Wider net, more stores.
- **Map view** shows the same stores as pins, colored by how reliable each store is.

The list does real work for you:

- **Best bets** float to the top. Those are the stores most likely to have genuine product at list price.
- Open and closed is computed live in each store's own time zone. We won't call a closed store.
- **"Drops Tuesday"** under a store means real calls taught us its shipment day.
- **"Coming soon"** means the store is on the map but not callable quite yet.

Searching a different town or ZIP is a [Check+ perk called Any town](doc:any-town). Free accounts search near their own location.
