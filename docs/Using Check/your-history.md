---
title: Your history
---
Every check you run is saved with its full conversation.

Open **Check history** from your account. It lands on today's newest check, and a calendar shows your past days glowing in the verdict's color: green for in stock, red for not in, yellow for unclear.

- Tap any day to reread that day's checks.
- Arrows on a result flip between checks like pages.
- Every saved check keeps the whole transcript, so you can always reread exactly what the store said.

The **Activity** tab in your account graphs your week and lets you filter by outcome.
