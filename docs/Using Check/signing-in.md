---
title: Signing in
---
No passwords. Ever.

You sign in with your **US phone number**. We text you a 6 digit code, you type it in, you're in. The code screen even fills itself when your phone offers the text.

Your first check is free the moment you sign up. No card needed.

A few details worth knowing:

- US numbers only for now.
- Same number = same account, always. Sign in on any device and your checks, history, and balance follow you.
- Didn't get the text? Tap **Resend**. Still nothing? Double check the number and try again in a minute.
- Signing out is in your account sheet, bottom row.
