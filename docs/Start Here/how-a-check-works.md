title: How a check works
---
Three steps. You barely lift a finger.

1. **Pick a store.** Tell us what you're hunting. We show the stores near you that stock it, closest first, with the open ones flagged.
2. **We call.** Check AI dials the store, skips past the hold music and the phone menu, and asks a real person the one question that matters. You can watch the conversation stream to your phone live.
3. **You get the verdict.** The moment the call wraps, you get a plain answer, color coded so you know before you even read it.
