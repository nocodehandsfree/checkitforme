Three steps. You barely lift a finger.

1. **Pick a store.** Tell us what you're hunting and where you are. We show the stores nearby that actually stock it, closest first, with the open ones flagged.
2. **We call.** Check AI dials the store, waits through the hold music and the phone menu, and asks a real person the one question that matters: *"Got any of it in right now?"* You can watch the conversation live, the transcript streaming to your phone as it happens.
3. **You get the verdict.** The moment the call wraps, you get a plain answer, color-coded so you know before you even read it.
