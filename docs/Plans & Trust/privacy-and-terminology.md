title: "Privacy & terminology"
hidden: false
---

We keep it lean. Sign-in is your phone number and a texted code, no passwords to leak. We store what's needed to place your checks and show your history, and nothing we don't use.

A couple of words we use carefully, so the app always reads the same way:

- **Check**, what you spend, and one call about one product.
- **Call**, the literal phone call we place to the store.
- **Check AI**, the voice that makes the call.
- **Staff**, the person who answers at the store.
