Your first check is free. After that, you choose how you spend:

- **Pay as you go**, buy a pack of checks, use them whenever. They don't expire.
- **A monthly plan**, a bundle of checks each month, plus extras like restock alerts, auto-checks on shipment days, and zone sweeps that check all your stores in one tap.
- **Check+**, the membership, for the people who hunt seriously.

Billing is handled entirely on our side and only ever fires on a real, answered verdict. You never get charged mid-call or on a maybe.
