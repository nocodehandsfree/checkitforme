---
title: Add your store
---
Know a store we're missing? Tell us and get paid in checks.

Account → Earn → **Add your store**: give us the store's name and city (a note helps). Our team verifies it, adds it to the map, and the moment it goes live you get **a free check** plus an email letting you know.

Your submissions live in the Earn tab with their status: **Pending review**, **Live 🎉**, or **Not added** (usually means we couldn't verify a phone number for it).

Card shops, comic shops, that one grocery store with a weirdly good card aisle: this is exactly how they get on the map.
