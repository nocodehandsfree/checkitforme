---
title: Kiosk receipts
---
Bought something at a Pokémon vending machine? That email receipt is worth **a free check.**

The machines email you a receipt when you buy. Forward it to the address shown in the app (Account → Earn → **Kiosk receipt**, tap to copy) and keep the app open. Within a minute or two we verify it and your free check lands.

Why we pay for these: a receipt is hard proof that machine was powered on, stocked, and selling at that exact minute. You're keeping the kiosk map honest for everyone.

Only real machine receipts count, and each receipt can only be claimed once.
