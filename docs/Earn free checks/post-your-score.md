title: Post your score
---
Check said in stock, you drove over, you got the box. **Post the win.**

The **Scores** wall is the community feed of real pickups: photo, what you got, which store came through. Open it from the footer or right from your in stock result ("Grabbed it? Post your score").

You can only post scores for stores where Check actually confirmed stock for you, which keeps the wall real: every post is a verified find, not a flex from three months ago.

Double tap to like. And yes, the wall is where everyone else finds out your store has good pulls, so post accordingly.
