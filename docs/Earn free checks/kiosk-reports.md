---
title: Kiosk reports
---
You watched the restock guy fill the machine at 10:40 on a Tuesday? That intel is worth **a free check.**

In kiosk mode, tap **Report kiosk timing** and tell us what you saw: when the machine gets refilled, how often, anything useful. We fold every report into that kiosk's refresh pattern, which makes "is it stocked right now?" smarter for the whole map.

The more specific the better. "Refills Tuesday and Friday mornings" beats "sometimes has stuff".
