---
title: Invite a friend
---
The best one on the board: **you get 3 free checks, they get 3 free checks.**

Open your account, hit the **Earn** tab, tap **Invite a friend**. You get a personal link to text, post, or share however you like.

When a friend signs up through your link, both of you get credited automatically. No codes to type, no waiting.

Fine print, kept short: one referral credit per new person (they have to be actually new), and you can invite as many friends as you want.
