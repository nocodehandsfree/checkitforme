title: "The life of a check"
hidden: false
---

Here's what actually happens between your tap and your verdict. It's more machinery than it looks.

1. **You start it.** You sign in with your phone number (a texted code, no passwords ever). You pick a store and a product, and that kicks off the check.
2. **We dial.** The call goes out over the real phone network, and it can even show the store your own verified number, so it's a normal local call, not a robocall.
3. **We get to a person.** Most stores answer with a menu or a phone tree. Check AI knows the fastest route through each one and works the menu to a human, without burning money doing it.
4. **We ask.** Once a person is on the line, Check AI asks the one stock question and listens. The whole exchange streams to your phone live.
5. **We read the answer twice.** Two independent reads decide the verdict. If they disagree, we don't guess, you get an honest "couldn't tell," and it's free.
