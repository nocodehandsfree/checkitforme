---
title: "Why reaching a person is the real cost"
hidden: false
---

A phone call is cheap. A phone call with a smart AI listening and talking the whole time is not, and while you're just sitting in a phone menu, that smart part is doing nothing useful but still running the meter.

So we keep it switched off until it's needed. Check AI navigates the menu with free keypad presses or cheap prerecorded speech, and the expensive, conversational part only wakes up **the moment a human actually answers**. The deeper the phone tree, the more we save, because those are exactly the minutes you'd otherwise be paying full price for.

> **Why it matters to you**
>
> This is what makes "no answer = no charge" affordable. We spend almost nothing getting to the person, so we can eat the cost of every call that doesn't reach one.
