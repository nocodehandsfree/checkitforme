The hard part isn't calling a store. It's getting past the phone menu to an actual person. Call a pharmacy yourself and you'll burn five minutes pressing buttons before anyone picks up.

So we mapped the phone systems of the national chains. Check AI knows the fastest route to a human before it even dials.

Here's the money trick: the smart, expensive part of Check AI stays asleep while we work the menu. It only wakes up the moment a real person answers. We spend almost nothing getting to the person.

That's not just nerd pride. It's what makes **no answer = no charge** affordable. Reaching a human costs us so little that we can eat every call that doesn't.
