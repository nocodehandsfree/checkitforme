title: "Proof, not guesses"
hidden: false
---

Stock-checker websites lie constantly, they say "in stock" when the shelf is bare, because their inventory feed is hours behind reality. A phone call to a person standing near the shelf isn't.

That's why every check comes with the **receipt**: the live transcript of the actual conversation. You see the question, you see the answer, you see the store say a shipment landed this morning. If we tell you it's in stock, you can read exactly why we think so.
