Stock checker websites lie constantly. Their feed says "in stock" while the shelf sits empty, because the data is hours behind reality.

A person standing near the shelf isn't.

That's why every check ships with the receipt: the transcript of the actual conversation. You see the question. You see the answer. You see the store say a shipment landed this morning. If we tell you it's in stock, you can read exactly why we think so.
