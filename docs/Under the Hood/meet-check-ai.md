Check AI is the voice that makes the call. It sounds like a friendly person calling around, because that's the job, a store associate is far more helpful to a real "hey, quick question" than to a robot.

The clever part is how it gets *to* that associate. Every store's phone system is one of three kinds, and Check AI handles each differently:

- **Direct**, a person just picks up. Nothing to navigate.
- **Keypad menu**, "press 3 for the store." We press the right keys automatically, for free.
- **Voice menu**, the kind that wants you to *say* "front desk." We speak the right words to get through, cheaply.

It learns each store's fastest path once and reuses it, so the calls get quicker and cheaper over time.
