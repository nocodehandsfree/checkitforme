title: Meet Check AI
---
Check AI is the voice that makes the call. It sounds like a friendly person calling around, because that's the job. Store staff are way more helpful to a real "heyy, quick question" than to a robot.

The clever part is how it gets to that person. Every store's phone system is one of three kinds, and Check AI handles each one:

- **Direct.** A person just picks up. Nothing to navigate.
- **Keypad menu.** "Press 3 for the store." We press the right keys the instant we hear the menu.
- **Voice menu.** The kind that wants you to say "front desk." We say the right words and get routed straight through.

It learns each store's fastest path once and reuses it on every call after that. The calls get quicker and cheaper over time.
