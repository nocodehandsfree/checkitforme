---
title: Restock alerts
---
Stop checking. Get told.

On any result you can tap **Tell me when it's back** and we watch that store for you. The moment we confirm product landed, you get pinged.

- **Email alerts** are unlimited on every plan.
- **Text alerts** are the premium ration, and each plan includes a monthly allowance: Family 5, Collector 15, Hunter 40, Operator 150 texts a month. (Texts are still rolling out carrier approval, so email is the reliable channel today.)

Alerts are one and done: we ping you, you go grab it, and the watch retires so you never get spammed about the same restock twice.
