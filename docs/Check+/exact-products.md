---
title: Exact products
---
Free checks ask the general question: "got any Pokémon in?"

With **Exact products**, one call asks for exactly what you're hunting. On the same call the agent finds out whether it's in, which set it is, and the product type, and you can hand it an example like "a Prismatic Evolutions ETB or booster box."

The real power is checking **more than one thing on a single call.** Done asking about your Pokémon at that store? The agent can roll right into your One Piece and your Topps NBA while it still has the person on the line. The stores that carry one of those usually carry the others, so one call, one check, can clear your whole list.

Why it matters: "we have Pokémon" often means a wall of commons. Asking for the exact set, across every game you collect, is how you skip the drive that ends in disappointment.
