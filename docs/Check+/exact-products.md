---
title: Exact products
---
Free checks ask the general question: "got any Pokémon in?"

With **Exact products**, your call asks for exactly what you're hunting: "any Prismatic Evolutions ETBs?" You pick the product from a dropdown before the call, and Check AI names it to the staff.

You can also select **more than one product for a single call**. One call, one check, several answers: the agent runs down your list with the person on the phone and the result breaks out each item.

Why it matters: "we have Pokémon" often means a wall of commons. Asking for the exact set is how you skip the drive that ends in disappointment.
