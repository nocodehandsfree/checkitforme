---
title: Your voice
---
The voice that calls stores for you can be yours to shape. **Your voice** personalizes your calling agent: pick the voice you like best for your checks, or go further and make it sound like you.

Combined with [Caller ID](doc:caller-id), your checks become almost indistinguishable from you calling the store yourself: your number on their screen, your kind of voice on the line, and you never had to dial.

> 🔜 **Rolling out.** The voice tech is live inside Check today (it's how we test call styles). The member facing picker is in testing and will switch on without an update.
