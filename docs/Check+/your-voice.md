---
title: Your voice
---
The voice that calls stores for you isn't one stiff robot. **Your voice** lets you pick the calling voice you like best for your checks, so the person who answers hears a friendly, natural caller.

Paired with [Caller ID](doc:caller-id), your checks already feel like you: your number on the store's screen, a warm voice on the line, and you never had to dial.

> 🔜 **Still growing.** Picking your calling voice is here. Making it sound like *you* is something we're testing carefully before we turn it on, so nobody gets a rushed version. This page updates the day it ships.
