---
title: Hobby hunts
---
For the collector who knows exactly what they want, down to the set.

**Hobby hunts** opens a browsable wall of Pokémon: every era, every set with its real art, every product form (booster box, ETB, bundle, tins) with its list price. New sets show up flagged "Just dropped"; announced ones show "Upcoming".

Pick your product and Check drops you into hunt mode at the card shops near you, with a banner locked to your target. Every call asks for that exact product.

Card shop reality check, so nobody's surprised: hobby shops almost always price above list. The list shows a **SHOP PRICES** tag so you know before you call. What you're buying at a card shop is selection, not a deal.

Pokémon only for now. It pairs perfectly with [Exact products](doc:exact-products).
