---
title: What Check+ is
---
Check+ is the membership. Every paid plan, from Family up, unlocks the same full set of power tools. Pay as you go covers the core move (call a store, get the answer) but doesn't include these.

| Perk | One line |
|---|---|
| [Exact products](doc:exact-products) | Ask for a specific set, and check several products in one call |
| [Zone sweeps](doc:zone-sweeps) | Check every store in an area with one tap |
| [Restock alerts](doc:restock-alerts) | Get pinged when a store restocks |
| [Scheduled checks](doc:scheduled-checks) | Auto check stores on their shipment days |
| [Any town](doc:any-town) | Hunt anywhere, not just near you |
| [Store holds](doc:store-holds) | Ask the store to hold one for you |
| [Your voice](doc:your-voice) | Personalize your calling agent's voice |
| [Thrift hunts](doc:thrift-hunts) | Hunt thrift stores and hobby shops |

Members also get [Hobby hunts](doc:hobby-hunts), the Pokémon set browser that hunts one exact product at card shops.

Pick a plan on the [Plans](doc:plans) page. Everything switches on the moment payment clears.
