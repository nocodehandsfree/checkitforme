---
title: Scheduled checks
---
Stores restock on a rhythm. Your checks can run on the same rhythm, without you.

From a result, tap **Auto check on shipment days**. We already know many stores' delivery days from real calls ("drops Tuesday"), so your auto check runs on exactly those mornings and the answer is waiting on your phone.

The rules:

- Each auto check spends one check from your balance, same as a manual one, and the same no answer = no charge protection applies.
- We only run it when the store is actually open.
- Every scheduled check you have lives in your account with a one tap **Cancel**.

Set it on the stores you trust, then let shipment day come to you.
