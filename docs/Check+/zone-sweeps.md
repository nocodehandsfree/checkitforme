---
title: Zone sweeps
---
One tap. Every store in the area gets checked.

A **zone** is a group of stores you save: "Stores near work", "Saturday route", whatever. Build one by searching a town or ZIP, or from the map, tick the stores you want, name it, save.

Then hit **Check all** and we call every store in the zone at once. A live report fills in as the calls land: green for in stock, red for not in, gray for no answer. Tap any store to read its conversation. You can stop the run mid sweep, and share the results when it's done.

The math stays fair:

- Each store in the sweep costs one check, and we make sure you have enough before starting.
- The no answer rule still applies per store. A store that never picks up doesn't spend a check.

Manage zones from your account: rename, edit stores, delete, re-run.
