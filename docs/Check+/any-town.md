---
title: Any town
---
Free accounts hunt near their own location. **Any town** takes the leash off.

Type any ZIP or city into the store search and the map jumps there. Hunting a store near grandma's house before Thanksgiving, scouting the town you're driving through Saturday, checking the city where a big drop hit: all fair game.

On the map you can also drop a pin anywhere and search around it.

Works everywhere Check has coverage, which is most of the country: 100,000+ stores and counting.
