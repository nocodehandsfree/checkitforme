Meet Check

Pokémon in stock? We'll check for you.

You want the box before it's gone. Driving store to store wastes gas. Calling around yourself is worse. So we do it.

You tap a store. Our AI phones it and talks to a real person. The answer lands on your phone in about two minutes.

Here's the game. A hot set drops and it's gone from every website in minutes. But it's sitting on a shelf near you right now, because a truck just dropped it and nobody's found it yet. Check calls the stores, so you find that shelf first.

It works for the stuff that actually sells out: Pokémon, One Piece, Topps Chrome NBA, and NeeDoh.

📗

The promise

First check's on us. And no answer = no charge. If we can't get you a clear yes or no, you don't spend a thing.
