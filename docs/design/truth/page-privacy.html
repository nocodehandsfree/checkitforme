Privacy Policy

Here's what Check It For Me (checkitforme.com) collects, why, and what you can do about it. Short version: we take only what we need to run your checks, we keep it only as long as we need it, and we never sell it.

What we collect

  • Your cell number. It's how you sign in, and we call stores on your behalf, so a verified number is required. No number, no checks.
  • Your checks. The store, the product, the result, and the written conversation we bring back as your proof.
  • Rough location. Only if you allow it, to show stores near you. Say no and search by ZIP instead.
  • Payment info. Stripe handles your card. We never see the full number.
  • Basic usage. Enough to keep the app running, fix bugs, and stop abuse.

How we use it

To place your calls, show your history, take payment, keep the service safe, and make the answers more accurate. That is the whole list.

Who we share it with

Only the vendors that make Check work, and only so they can do their job. Our AI voice provider places and transcribes the calls. Twilio sends your login codes and alert texts, and carries the calls. Stripe processes payments. They handle your info under their own terms, on our behalf. We do not sell your personal information, and we never will.

How long we keep it

We keep your account, your checks, and their conversations until you ask us to delete them, or until we no longer need them to run the service. Delete your account and we remove your personal data, apart from the little the law requires us to hold.

Your rights

You can see your data, correct it, download a copy, or delete it. Turn off location any time in your browser. Turn off alert texts and emails from your account. Want a copy or a deletion? Ask us in the app chat or on Discord and we will take care of it.

Kids

Check is made for adults in the United States. It is not meant for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.
