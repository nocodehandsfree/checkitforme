We don't just have a list of stores. We have a **dossier on each one**, and it's what makes a check land in two minutes instead of a wasted afternoon.

For a single location, we track:

- **A direct phone line** that reaches the store, not a national call center.
- **Real hours**, so we know if it's open *right now*, computed in the store's own time zone.
- **Exact location**, address, ZIP, and map pin, so "near me" is actually near you.
- **What it carries**, which collectible lines that store actually stocks.
- **A reliability tier**, how good a bet it is for genuine product at list price.
- **Kiosk status**, whether an official Pokémon vending machine is on site.
- **Its restock rhythm**, the day of the week it usually gets card shipments, learned from real calls.
- **A map of its phone system**, the exact route through the menu to reach a human fast.

**Over 105,000 stores across 120+ retail chains, about 97% of the shelves that carry Pokémon at MSRP.** And it's not just Pokémon, the same map knows which stores stock every line we cover:

| Product | Stores that carry it |
|---|---:|
| Pokémon | 102,142 |
| Topps Chrome | 70,296 |

By store type, the depth is just as real: **38,275 discount** stores, **15,352 pharmacies**, **6,356 big-box**, **1,227 craft & hobby**, and on down the list, each one with its own hours, phone line, and reliability score.

That's not a scraped directory. It's a living map that gets more accurate every time we place a call.
