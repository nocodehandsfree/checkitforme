---
title: Store ratings
---
One question decides the rating: how good is your chance of finding genuine product at list price, right now?

| Tier | You see | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **5** | 🟢 Best Chance | Genuine, list price, and a restock rhythm you can time. Every kiosk store too. |
| **4** | 🟣 Mostly Reliable | Genuine and list price, but no dependable rhythm. |
| **3** | 🟡 Spotty | List price, but it varies store to store. |
| **2** | ⚪ Over MSRP | Has it, but above list price. Card shops and resellers live here. |

It's a best chance, never a promise. A small store that restocks like clockwork beats a big one that's random.
