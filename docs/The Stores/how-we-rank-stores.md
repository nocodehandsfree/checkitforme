hidden: false
---

The rating answers one question: *how reliably is it worth checking this store for genuine product at MSRP right now?* It's a best chance, never a promise. A small, predictable restocker beats a big random one.

| Tier | What you see | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **5** | 🟢 Best Chance | Genuine MSRP, carries broadly, restock rhythm you can time. Plus every kiosk store. |
| **4** | 🟣 Mostly Reliable | Genuine MSRP and broad, but no dependable rhythm. |
| **3** | 🟡 Spotty | Genuine MSRP but varies store to store. |
| **2** | ⚪ Over MSRP | Has it, but above list price, card shops, GameStop, resellers. |

There's a separate thing we track that never shows up as a star rating: **reach rate**, whether a human actually picks up the phone. A Barnes & Noble answers direct; a pharmacy hides behind a phone bot. That's about how hard the store is to reach, not how good the shelf is, and it's what we quietly work to get faster.
