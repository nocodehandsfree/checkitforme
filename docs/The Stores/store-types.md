---
title: Store types
---
Not every store is the same kind of bet. The type tells you what you're walking into.

- **Retail.** Target, Walmart, CVS, Barnes & Noble. Sealed, genuine product at or near list price. The best value hunt.
- **Card shop.** Local hobby shops. Real sealed product, almost always above list price. Great selection, and you pay for it.
- **Thrift.** Goodwill and friends. A treasure hunt for loose cards from old collections. Spotty by nature, so it lives as its own separate adventure.
- **Kiosk.** The official Pokémon vending machines. A machine sets the price, so it's always exact list price.

Under the hood these roll up from a much finer grid: pharmacy, grocery, warehouse club, electronics, dollar stores, and more. That's how we point you at the right shelf instead of just the nearest one.
