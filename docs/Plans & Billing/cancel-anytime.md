---
title: Cancel anytime
---
No hoops, no retention script, no "call us to cancel."

**Manage plan** in your account → cancel. That's it.

What happens to your stuff:

- **Pay as you go checks stay yours.** They never expire, cancelled or not.
- **Plan checks** stop refilling at the end of the period you already paid for.
- Your history, account, and number all stay. Come back any time and it's all there.
- Check+ perks (alerts, zones, scheduled checks) switch off when the paid period ends.

Downgrading instead of cancelling works the same way and keeps your perks at the smaller plan's level.
