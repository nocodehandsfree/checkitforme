We keep it lean. You sign in with your phone number and a texted code. No passwords to leak.

We store what's needed to place your checks and show your history. Nothing we don't use.

And the words we use, so the app always reads the same way:

- **Check.** What you spend. One call about one product.
- **Call.** The literal phone call we place to the store.
- **Check AI.** The voice that makes the call.
- **Staff.** The person who answers at the store.
