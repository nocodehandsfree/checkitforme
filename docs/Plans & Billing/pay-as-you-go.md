---
title: Pay as you go
---
No subscription? Buy a bundle of checks. The balance never expires, and the price per check drops as you buy more.

| Bundle | Price | Per check |
|---|---|---|
| 10 checks | $9.90 | 99¢ |
| 25 checks | $19.99 | 80¢ |
| 50 checks | $34.99 | 70¢ |
| 75 checks | $47.99 | 64¢ |
| 100 checks | $60.00 | 60¢ |

Bundles cover the core move: call a store, check the shelf. [Check+ features](doc:what-check-plus-is) stay subscription only.
