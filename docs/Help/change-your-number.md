---
title: Change your number
---
Your phone number is your account, so changing it is a real move, and it's built in.

Open your account sheet and tap the **pencil** next to your number. Enter the new number, we text it a code, you confirm, done. Your balance, plan, history, and zones all come with you.

Two things to know:

- The new number has to be a US number you can receive texts on right now.
- If you set up [Caller ID](doc:caller-id), verify it again on the new number so your checks keep dialing as you.

Lost access to the old number entirely and can't sign in? Contact us and we'll sort you out.
