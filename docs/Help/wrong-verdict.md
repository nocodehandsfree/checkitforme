---
title: Wrong verdict
---
Drove over on a green light and the shelf was bare? Two honest truths and a fix.

The truths:

1. A check is a moment in time. "In stock at 2 PM" can be gone by 4 on a hot set. That's the game we're all playing.
2. Sometimes staff are just wrong about their own shelf. The transcript shows exactly what they told us, so you can always see whether we heard it right.

The fix:

- Read the conversation on the result. If the verdict doesn't match what the store actually said, tap the buttons under it (In stock / Not in / Restocking / Unclear) and tell us what you saw.
- Every disagreement gets reviewed by a human and corrects the record. It genuinely trains the system: your taps make the next thousand verdicts sharper.

And remember the pricing rule protects you here too: an unclear answer is never charged. You only ever pay when the store gave a straight answer.
